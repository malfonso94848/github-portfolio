About Me

There are many things to say but not enough words to express it. I am a very open minded individual, not afraid to accept other opinions or handle tasks in new and constructive ways. I believe there are always ways to improve myself or to improve the overall community through consideration and collaboration. Lastly, I strive for creativity, welcoming such forms in problem solving and personal lives. After all, to explore these endless possibilities is to find new ways forward and to express oneself in fascinating ways otherwise not possible. Applying to the ever-evolving technological world now more than ever. It was these principles of mine which have led me down my path as a Digital/Web Designer ever since High School. When I first stepped into this field, I was enraptured by this medium as nothing quite captured the involvement of creativity and my interest in the technological world as this career allows. Even triumphing over my years of experience within mediums of music, acting, writing and physical art of which I too thoroughly love. It has since been my goal to not only learn all I can within Digital Design but to use the skills and technology available to me to change the lives of others. To use the languages of scripting and the path of code in order to help those establish connections and create visions for idea's they cannot themselves. To aid others not as adept to not only introduce them, but guide them into a new world for the growth of businesses, establishment of products, or to simply help create their dreams within the ever growing wide web. To this day, I continue to strive and hone my skills and abilities within my education. Soon, the business world as well for the sake of innovation, exploration, and the building of these ever-important connections be that from the everyday man or woman to the products and/or features that may change their lives in small or big ways. Though I still have much to learn, life is about improving ourselves no matter what. That's why I won't stop learning even after I've become an Expert Web Designer in my field.
